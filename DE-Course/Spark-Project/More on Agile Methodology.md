### Agile

Sprints ( 2 weeks)

SCRUM (a project management technique to deliver a project in agile manner)

There are 4 different types of people in the project

1. Product Owner (Project Manager)
2. Scrum Master (plays a role of driving the things)
3. Developer
4. Stakeholders

scrum team is (1,2,3)

whenever a project comes

Lending Club - Risk Analysis

epic 1 - Develop a quick prototype
epic 2 - Make it production ready
epic 3 - Improve the performance

Product Owner who is responsible to create Backlogs

The product owner talks to various people and comes up with stories and adds them as part of Backlogs.

1. Sprint Planning - The product owner brings the product backlog to discuss with the development team. The scrum team does effort on story point estimation. The product backlog must contain all the details necessary for this estimation.
	Attended By - The Dev team, Scrum Master, Product Owner
	At the beginning of each Sprint - 2 hours meeting

2. Daily Stand-up - 10 mins call, a very quick call.
	blockers, what have you worked on yesterday and what are you planning to work on today. Generally happens in morning.
	Attended By - Dev Team, Scrum Master, Product Owner (optional)

3. Sprint Review - Showcase the teams completed work and gather feedback from stakeholders. Scrum Master drives this.
	Attended By - Dev Team, Product Owner, Scrum Master, Stakeholders (management, customers). 1 hour meeting

 4. Sprint Retrospective - you look back on work that has been done and in this sprint. What went well and what could have been done better.
	 Attended By - Dev Team, Scrum Master, and Product Owner. 1 hour meeting
